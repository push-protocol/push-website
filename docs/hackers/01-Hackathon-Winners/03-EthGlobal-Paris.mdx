# EthGlobal Paris 2023

[ETHGlobal Paris](https://ethglobal.com/events/paris2023), a beacon for Web3 innovation, gathered developers and creators at the Palais Brongniart this July for a hackathon dedicated to Ethereum's ecosystem. It featured workshops, expert talks, and industry partners like Polygon and Metamask. Push Protocol sponsored **$10,000 in bounties**, fostering a hub of past creativity.

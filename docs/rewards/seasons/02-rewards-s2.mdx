Multipliers allow users to boost their Points by performing special activities. For e.g. if you have 100 points, after earning a 2x Multiplier, you will now have 200 Points. Multipliers also apply to any points earned after activating the Multiplier making it a permanent feature. Watch out [@PushChain on X](https://x.com/PushChain) for bonus activities to earn Multipliers.
